# Pattern-QHP — Production Pilot

CORTI W204-PILOT — first production-grade end-to-end demo built on the
`slot-math-engine-template` stack. Phoenix-theme slot showcasing every

## Game description

> Soar with the Phoenix and reignite ancient power. Quick Hit reel-bound
> mystery flames reveal hidden symbols on every spin. Land three Phoenix
> Eggs to unlock the Phoenix Wing Free Spins round with retrigger and
> sticky multiplier ladder, or collect six Phoenix Orbs to ignite the
> 4-tier Hold & Win bonus — Mini, Minor, Major, or the legendary Grand
> jackpot worth 5,000× your bet.

Theme: **Phoenix / Fire / Sun / Lava**, palette anchored on `#FF6F1F`
flame orange + `#FFEA00` ember gold + `#7C1F23` ember red.

## Math features

| Feature | Mechanic | Contribution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Base game | 5×3 grid, 25 paylines, lines evaluation, LTR | 53% RTP |
| Quick Hit mystery (M5) | Mystery Flame reveals one symbol per spin | weighted toward MP/LP |
| Free Spins | 3+ scatters award 10/15/25 spins, ×2 global multiplier | 24% RTP |
| FS retrigger | 3+ scatters in FS adds 5/10/15 spins (max 50) | included above |
| FS modifiers | `multiplier_ladder`, `mystery_symbol` | sticky climb |
| Hold & Win | 6+ Orb symbols trigger 3 respins, orbs add cash + 4-tier jackpot | 14% RTP |
| Jackpot ladder | Mini 25×, Minor 100×, Major 500×, **Grand 5,000×** (full grid) | 5% RTP |

**Variance:** HIGH (volatility tag `high`)
**Max win cap:** 5,000× per spin (cap_apply: `per_spin`)
**Hit frequency:** 28% target / 28.1% measured (1M-spin MC).

node scripts/cert-pilot-flow.mjs --quick

`reports/pilot/QUICK_HIT_PLATINUM_PHOENIX.{json,md}`.
