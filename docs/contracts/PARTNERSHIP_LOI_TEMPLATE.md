# Strategic Partnership Letter of Intent (Template)

**Wave:** W214 Faza 1100.0 — Post-Pitch Negotiation Toolkit  
**Document type:** Sales-engineering reference template  
**Status:** DRAFT — counsel review required before execution

This Letter of Intent ("LOI") expresses the **non-binding** intention
of `{{operator_name}}` ("Operator") and `{{vendor_legal_entity}}`
("Vendor") to explore a strategic partnership ("Partnership") in the
slot-math-engine category. This LOI is an alternative to a full
acquisition (Tier C) or a straight license (Tier A/B), and is
typically pursued when both Parties believe a deeper alignment can
unlock category leadership.

---

## 1. Mutual Interest

The Parties have completed (or will complete) a successful Pilot per
`PILOT_AGREEMENT_TEMPLATE.md` and have determined that a deeper
collaboration is in their mutual strategic interest. The Parties
acknowledge:

- Operator brings distribution, brand, regulatory relationships in
  `{{operator_jurisdictions}}`, and a deep portfolio of titles.
- Vendor brings the closed-form solver portfolio, the IR
  specification, the certification dossier toolchain, and a velocity
  advantage in math iteration.
- Combining these strengths under a structured Partnership is
  expected to deliver faster time-to-cert, higher hit-rate on title
  launches, and category lock-in against competitors.

## 2. Proposed Structure

The Parties propose a Partnership organized as follows:

1. **Joint Go-To-Market.** Co-branded sales efforts across Operator's
   jurisdictions, with mutual right of first refusal on
   Operator-marketed titles built on the Engine.
2. **Revenue share on jointly developed titles.** A baseline 50/50
   split of net game revenue on titles co-developed under the
   Partnership, with adjustment formulas for outsized contribution
   (e.g., Operator-funded marketing, Vendor-funded custom math).
3. **Co-branding.** Engine-powered titles may carry a co-branded mark
   (e.g., "Powered by `{{vendor_brand}}` × `{{operator_brand}}`")
   subject to brand-team approval on creative.
4. **Steering committee.** Two representatives from each Party plus a
   neutral chair (rotating annually) to govern roadmap and disputes.

## 3. Exclusivity

The Parties grant each other a **12-month exclusivity option**:

- Operator: will not enter another similar partnership with a
  competing slot-math-engine vendor during the exclusivity window.
- Vendor: will not enter another similar partnership with a competing
  Tier-1 operator in `{{operator_jurisdictions}}` during the window.

Exclusivity does not restrict either Party's existing arms-length
license or vendor relationships outside the Partnership scope.

## 4. Resource Commitments

### 4.1 Vendor Commitments

- `{{vendor_engineering_hours}}` engineering hours allocated to
  Partnership work in the first 12 months.
- Dedicated math TPM as primary point of contact.
- Engine roadmap input slot on Operator's executive review cadence.

### 4.2 Operator Commitments

- `{{operator_titles_committed}}` titles committed to ship on the
  Engine in the first 12 months.
- Dedicated distribution slot in Operator's prime release windows.
- Co-marketing budget of `{{operator_marketing_usd}}` USD over the
  first 12 months.

## 5. Milestones

| Milestone               | Month | Definition                                                                   |
| :---------------------- | ----: | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Foundation              |     3 | LOI converted to MoU; first co-developed title in math validation            |
| First launch            |     6 | At least one (1) Engine-powered title live in `{{operator_jurisdictions}}`   |
| Portfolio momentum      |    12 | At least 25% of Operator's annual ships on the Engine; revenue share active  |
| Strategic review        |    18 | Joint review of category position; decision on Tier C (acquisition) path     |

Failure to meet the 12-month milestone triggers an automatic
re-negotiation window of 90 days; failure at the 18-month review
triggers an unwind option for either Party.

## 6. Performance Guarantees

By the end of Month 12, the Partnership target is for **at least 25%**
of Operator's annual game ships in scope (excluding live-dealer or
non-slot products) to be built on the Engine, with a stretch goal of
**40%**. Achievement of the stretch goal triggers an accelerator
clause: Vendor receives a one-time bonus equal to
`{{stretch_bonus_usd}}` USD or the equivalent in additional revenue
share points (Operator election).

## 7. Negotiation Timeline

The Parties intend to progress the Partnership as follows:

1. **LOI signed** — `{{loi_effective_date}}` (Month 0)
2. **Memorandum of Understanding** — Month 1 (binding on major terms,
   non-binding on operational detail)
3. **Definitive Agreement** — Month 3 (full MSA per
   `MSA_TEMPLATE.md`, plus Partnership rider)
4. **Steering committee live** — Month 4
5. **First co-developed title in market** — Month 6

## 8. Path to Acquisition

If, by the 18-month strategic review, the Partnership exceeds the
Month-12 stretch goal (>40% portfolio penetration) or generates
attributable Engine-related revenue of `{{acquisition_trigger_usd}}`
USD or more (trailing 12 months), the Parties shall enter good-faith
acquisition discussions per `ACQUISITION_DEAL_MEMO_TEMPLATE.md`.
Operator shall have a 60-day right of first refusal at fair market
value, established by a mutually-selected independent valuation firm.

## 9. Confidentiality and No-Shop

This LOI is governed by the Mutual NDA between the Parties dated
`{{nda_effective_date}}`. Additionally, for a period of sixty (60)
days following the LOI effective date, neither Party shall solicit or
negotiate a competing partnership with the same scope (the "No-Shop
Period").

## 10. Non-Binding Nature

This LOI is **non-binding** except for: (a) Section 3 (Exclusivity)
during the 12-month exclusivity window, (b) Section 9 (Confidentiality
and No-Shop) for the No-Shop Period, and (c) standard provisions on
expenses (each Party bears its own) and governing law.

Definitive obligations are subject to execution of the MoU and the
definitive agreement.

## 11. Governing Law

This LOI shall be governed by `{{governing_law}}`. Disputes shall be
resolved by good-faith negotiation, escalating to mediation in
`{{governing_law_venue}}` if unresolved within 30 days.
