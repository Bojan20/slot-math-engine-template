> Send within 48 hours of a detractor NPS or high+ churn-risk alert.

**Subject:** Want to make sure we're getting this right — {{customer_name}}

**Audience:** Sponsor (only)

**Cadence:** Triggered by W215 churn-risk severity = high or critical

---

Hi {{primary_contact_first_name}},

I'm reaching out directly because the signals we monitor on our side
indicate we may not be fully meeting your expectations. Specifically:

- {{trigger_1}}
- {{trigger_2}}
- {{trigger_3}}

That's important to us, and I'd like to get on a call this week to
hear, in your own words, where we're falling short and what we can do
to fix it.

I'm not bringing slides. I'm bringing a notebook and our VP of
Customer Success ({{vp_name}}). The goal is to listen.

Suggested slots:

- {{slot_1}}
- {{slot_2}}
- {{slot_3}}

If none of those work, reply with any time that's easier and we will
move things to make it happen.

— {{csm_name}}

---

**Internal notes:**
- This template is intentionally short and human. Do not embellish.
- Brief the VP Customer Success before the call.
- Capture the call in a structured "save plan" with concrete commitments
  and review them weekly until the risk score drops below `medium`.
- If the score does not improve in 60 days, escalate to CEO awareness.
