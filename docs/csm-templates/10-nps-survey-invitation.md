**Audience:** All named contacts at the tenant

---

I'm asking you to take **60 seconds** to rate us on a scale of 0-10:
how likely are you to recommend the L&W slot-math platform to a
colleague at another operator?

Please click here to respond — the link expires in 14 days:

> {{nps_survey_url}}

can do more of it. If your score is **0-6**, I'd be especially grateful
for a comment so we can fix what's not working.

This goes directly to our customer-success team and rolls up into the
quarterly review we share with our leadership.

Thanks for your time.

— {{csm_name}}
