**Subject:** Heads-up — renewal conversation for {{customer_name}}

**Audience:** Sponsor + procurement contact (if known)

**Cadence:** T-90 days before `first_renewal_due`

---

Hi {{primary_contact_first_name}},

Want to give you the courtesy of a heads-up: your initial 12-month
agreement on the platform comes up for renewal on **{{renewal_date}}**
({{days_to_renewal}} days from today).

Before we get into commercials, I'd love to have an exploratory
conversation about:

1. What's working vs not working with the current package
2. Volume forecasts for the next 12 months (we can right-size pricing)
3. Any expansions on the table (new jurisdictions, new game families,
   new operator brands under your group)
4. Your procurement window — when do contracts typically need to
   land for {{customer_name}}'s budget cycle?

This is *not* a commercial pitch. The goal is to be a partner in your
planning, not to spring a 30-day window on you.

Could we grab 30 minutes between {{date_window_start}} and
{{date_window_end}}? My calendar: {{calendar_link}}.

— {{csm_name}}
