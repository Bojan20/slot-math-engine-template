# Cold CEO — Email (formal)

> Summary line 1: Reserved-for-CEO cold outbound, only after CTO/CMO/CFO outreach has stalled.
> Summary line 2: Tone: formal, strategic, board-level framing — no engineering, no marketing.
> Summary line 3: Goal: route to right C-level for follow-up, OR a 15-min "do we need to talk?" call.

## Tone notes
- CEOs of public companies receive 100+ cold emails per week. Most get archived.
- The only signal that gets through: a board-relevant, single-page, no-ask-yet thesis.
- Do NOT request a demo, a meeting, or a pilot in the first email. Request a routing decision.
- Use full sentences; no bullets in the v1 body.
- ≤170 words.
- Use the formal greeting "Dear {{title}} {{last_name}}", not first-name.

## Subject line variants
- A: "Acceleration of studio cadence — board-level read on math engine"
- B: "Strategic note: 8 ships → 24 ships per year, same headcount"
- C: "Vendor B acceleration thesis — one page, no follow-up commitment"

## Body
Dear {{title}} {{last_name}},

I lead engineering on a math kernel platform that compresses the math + lab-cert stage of a slot title from twelve weeks to fourteen days. The platform covers all sixteen of Vendor B's published mechanic families closed-form, has paper-trail compatibility with BMM, GLI, eCOGRA, and NMi, and ships under three commercial pathways (acquire, license, pilot).

I am not asking for a meeting in this note. I am asking whether this thesis is worth a board-level conversation at Vendor B, and if so, which member of your C-suite should own it. We have already prepared one-pager, deep-dive, and ROI artifacts for the CTO, CMO, and CFO views — I am happy to send all three to whichever person you nominate.

If the thesis is not relevant for Vendor B at this point in the cycle, equally happy to hear that — your time is better spent on other items.

Respectfully,
{{sender_name}}
{{sender_title}}
{{sender_email}} | {{sender_phone}} | {{sender_link}}

## Send checklist
- [ ] Title and last name are correct (verify spelling on company bio page)
- [ ] No links in v1 body — they will reduce reply rate from C-suite
- [ ] Sent before 9:00 target-local (CEOs read inbox 6:30–8:30)
- [ ] Sender email is a custom domain, not Gmail
- [ ] Phone in signature is monitored (CEOs may call back, not email)

## Follow-up trigger
- 7 days no response → ONE polite nudge (same template, "circling back")
- Routed to a specific C-level → use that role's `cold-*-email.md` as a warm intro
- Direct reply asking for materials → respond same-day with one-pager + deep-dive

## CEO-specific caution
- Never use this template before exhausting CTO/CMO/CFO paths.
- If CEO replies, the deal velocity will be set by their attention span — be ready with all artifacts staged for instant send.
- Do not over-share in the first reply; let them ask.
