# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are a small project focused on rigorous slot math engineering.
Reasonable expectations apply:

* **Be technically rigorous.** Math claims need a derivation or a test
  fixture. Performance claims need a Criterion benchmark.
* **Be specific.** "It's slow" is unhelpful; "the `cluster_pays` kernel
  takes 270 ns on Apple M-series, target is < 200 ns" is helpful.
* **Be civil.** Critique the code, not the contributor.
* **Be patient.** This is a small project; reviews may take days.

## Unacceptable

* Personal attacks, harassment, doxxing
* Spam / off-topic promotion
* Submitting LLM-generated content as your own without disclosure
* Submitting acceptance fixtures without rebuilding the Merkle (this
  breaks the audit trail and will be reverted)

## Enforcement

Project lead: Boki Petković (<bojan.petkovic25@gmail.com>).

Violations may result in:
1. Comment removal + warning
2. Temporary block from issue / PR participation
3. Permanent ban for repeated violations

Decisions are final but you may appeal once via email.
