Pricing FAQ

1. What's the 30-day pilot?

Platform & Enterprise tiers include a 30-day pilot. We port one of your existing titles to our IR, run closed-form reconciliation, and submit a dry-run dossier to your chosen lab. If you walk away, you owe nothing.

2. Why a revenue share on top of subscription?

The fixed fee covers the engine + cert paper trail. The rev share aligns us with your shipping cadence — if you don't ship, we don't earn it.

3. Can I switch tiers mid-year?

Yes. Upgrade any time, pro-rated. Downgrades take effect at renewal. Your IR + cert dossier are 100% portable across tiers.

4. What happens to my games if I leave?

Your IR files and audit log are exported on request. The closed-form kernels stay licensed for 90 days post-termination so you can finish pending cert submissions. Enterprise customers get source escrow.

5. Is there a custom mechanic surcharge?

Enterprise includes 3 custom solvers/year. Platform pays a one-time $15K per new solver outside the 16 L&W mechanics, which becomes part of the public catalog (you keep first-mover rights for 6 months).

6. Do you support our existing wallet provider?

9 are pre-plumbed. We custom-build a new wallet adapter at $35K one-time (Platform) or free (Enterprise) — it lands in the public catalog the same week.

7. How is data protected?

All tenant data is hash-chain audited with Ed25519 signing. Per-tenant PG isolation. GDPR-compliant. SOC 2 Type II underway (target Q4 2026).

8. What's the cancellation policy?

30 days written notice. No early-termination fee. We pre-stage the data export at signup so churn is friction-less for you.
