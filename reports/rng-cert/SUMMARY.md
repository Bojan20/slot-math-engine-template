roll-up across both layers of evidence:

to qualify (uniformity is what matters, not centrality).

External tools are queued manually because they require 8–48 hours
per backend on commodity hardware. They are not part of the per-PR
CI gate; CI runs the internal battery as a regression check, lab
submissions ship the external reports separately. This split is
explicit in the runbook (P0 #3 done at engine layer).

The `scripts/cert-bundle.sh` script wraps a 12 MiB raw-stream dump
per backend, the manifest, and a hardware fingerprint — sufficient
for first-pass GLI-19 § 3.3.2 reproducibility checks. Full lab
submission requires running the External-Tools row above and
attaching their reports.

## Acceptance criteria

For a backend to ship into a regulator submission:

1. Internal battery: all 8 NIST sub-tests pass at 16 MiB.
2. PractRand: at least 256 GiB consumed without `unusual` or
   `suspicious` flags. (16 GiB minimum for non-CSPRNG; 256 GiB
   recommended for CSPRNG to assert long-period quality.)
3. TestU01 SmallCrush: 0 failures across the 15 batteries.
4. NIST STS: ≥ 96 % of sub-tests pass at p ≥ 0.01 across all 15
   suites (NIST allowance for stochastic test variance).
5. Hardware fingerprint matches the platform the lab will replay on
   (architecture / OS major / rustc version pinned in
   `rust-toolchain.toml`).

ChaCha20 satisfies (1) ✅ as of this commit. (2)–(4) require the
external runs queued from the runbook; the submission bundle layer
is ready to ship them once captured.

## Verification commands

```
# Re-run the internal battery (≤ 5 s per backend on M3 Pro):
for r in mulberry32 pcg64 xoshiro256ss philox4x32 chacha20; do
  cargo run --release --bin rng_cert -- \
    --mode internal --rng $r --seed 12345 --bytes 16777216 \
    --out reports/rng-cert/${r}-internal.json
done

# Rebuild the full submission bundle (defaults: 12 MiB per backend):
scripts/cert-bundle.sh --out reports/cert-bundle-current

# Stream raw bytes to a third-party tool:
cargo run --release --bin rng_cert -- --mode stream \
  --rng chacha20 --seed 12345 --bytes 4294967296 > /tmp/chacha20-4GB.bin
RNG_test stdin64 < /tmp/chacha20-4GB.bin    # PractRand
```
